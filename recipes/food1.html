Stir Fried Minced Pork with Fried Egg

"Pad Krao Pao" is one of the most popular and easy to prepare dishes from Thai cuisine. Holy basil (called Bai Krapow) is stir-fried along with a source of protein (stuffed meat, fried fish or tofu), garlic, chilli peppers and other typical Thai flavourings. Sometimes green beans or spring onions are also added.

Ingredients

  • 5-10 Thai Chillies
  • 5 Cloves Garlic
  • 0.5 Small Onion, Diced
  • 300g Chicken, coarsely ground
  • 1 Tbsp Oyster Sauche
  • 1 Tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp Fish Sauce
  • 1.5 tsp Black Soy Sauce
  • 1.5 tsp Sugar
  • 1.5 cup Holy Basil Leaves
  • Jasmine Rice for serving
  • Prik Nam Pla

Steps

  1. Pound Thai Chillies into a fine paste.
  2. Add Garlic and Spur Chilies and pound into a rough paste.
  3. Combine Oyster Sauce, Soy Sauce, Fish Sauce, Water, and Sugar; stir to dissolve the Sugar.
  4. n a wok or a large saute pan, saute the garlic-chili paste in a little vegetable oil over medium high heat until the garlic starts to turn golden.
  5. Add Chicken and toss until they're no longer in big clumps. Add the sauce and continue tossing until the chicken is almost done.
  6. Add Onions and Long Beans; toss until the chicken is done
  7. Remove from heat and stir in the Holy Basil.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  9. Serve the stir-fry over rice and top it with Fried Egg. Drizzle a little of the Prik Nam Pla over the egg and Bon Appetit!
